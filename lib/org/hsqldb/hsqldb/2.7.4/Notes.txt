This is the production hsqldb-2.7.4 jar with one manifest update:

Sealed: false

This allows Qortium's custom HSQLDBPool and monitoring classes to live in
org.hsqldb.jdbc without sealed-package failures.

Verify the patch with:

tools/rebuild-hsqldb-jar.sh --verify

The script downloads the official Maven Central jar and confirms that this jar
differs only by the manifest seal line changing from Sealed: true to
Sealed: false.
